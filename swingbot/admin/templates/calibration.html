What is calibration?

Calibration asks one question: does a higher confidence score actually win more? Every closed trade is bucketed into a decile by its confidence score at entry, and each decile's realized win rate is plotted against the 80% target line below. A well-calibrated system produces a roughly upward-sloping staircase — top-decile trades should win noticeably more often than bottom-decile trades. A flat or inverted staircase means the score isn't actually predictive yet and needs revisiting before it's trusted for sizing or filtering decisions.
